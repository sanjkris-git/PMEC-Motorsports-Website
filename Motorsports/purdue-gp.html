PGP Karts

Our organization proudly fields four entries - Kart 4, Kart 2, Kart 24, and Kart 26 - in the highly competitive annual Purdue Grand Prix, deemed "The Greatest Spectacle in College Racing." This demanding 160-lap, 50-mile endurance race, held every spring on the technical 0.27-mile Grand Prix Track, attracts 75 teams and over 3,500 dedicated spectators. Our karts are meticulously engineered to compete with absolute passion and precision against top organizations across the Purdue system, pushing the limits of driver skill and mechanical innovation.




Halloween 100

Our team is dedicated to competing in the Halloween Hundred, the spirited 100-lap race that marks a key second event on the Purdue Grand Prix calendar. Also held at the challenging Grand Prix Track in West Lafayette, this unique fall endurance contest allows us to showcase our technical prowess and on-track discipline outside of the main spring event. The race provides a high-stakes environment for driver development and crucial testing of our chassis setups. We approach the Halloween Hundred with the same level of commitment as the Grand Prix, focused on maximizing performance and securing a dominant finish. Adding to the competitive nature of this event, our team proudly enters the 2026 Halloween Hundred as the Defending Champion.

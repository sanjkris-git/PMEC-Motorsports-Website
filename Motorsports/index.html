About Us

The Purdue Motorsports Engineering Club, based in Indianapolis, is the largest student organization on campus, with over 125+ active members united by a passion for motorsports and engineering. We bring together students from diverse disciplines to design, build, and compete in racing events such as the Purdue Grand Prix and autocross. From concept to competition, we push the limits of performance and innovation, giving students real-world experience in areas like vehicle dynamics, data acquisition, fabrication, and race strategy — preparing them for careers in motorsport and the broader automotive industry. Whether you're a racing enthusiast or an aspiring engineer, there's a place for you here!

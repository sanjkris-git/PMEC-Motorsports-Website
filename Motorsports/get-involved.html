Dallara Partnership

Purdue Motorsports Engineering Club is fully integrated into Dallara's U.S. Headquarters in Speedway, Indiana, a state-of-the-art facility that houses the team's workshop and engineering infrastructure. This immersion allows us to engineer, fabricate, and race with the precision and operational excellence demanded by top-tier professional motorsports.
